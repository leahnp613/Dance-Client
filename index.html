Welcome to Lexington School of Ballet!

Celebrating 39 Years at the Munroe Center for the Arts in Lexington

Our faculty is excited to share the elegance of classical ballet, exuberance of jazz, and creativity of modern dance with all of our students. Through careful and  thoughtful  training for every dancer we aim to inspire artistry, grace, and musicality, while fostering confidence, friendship, and cooperative learning in a stimulating environment. Please join us at Lexington School of Ballet and dance!

 

2023 Winter/Spring Term
January 30 - June 17, 2023

Weekly classes for ages 4 through adult in Pre-Ballet, Ballet, Jazz, and Modern.

Note: The Fall/Winter Term continues through January 28, 2023 following our winter break from December 19 - January 2.
